My favorite beer style

IPA

Session IPA

It's not a Full Fat IPA, nor is it a Pale Ale, nor a British-styled sessionable pale beer, instead it fills in the middle of a hoppy Venn diagram of those three beer types. Reduce the strength in an IPA, lose some of the maltier middle of a Pale Ale, load up the hops on a golden ale, dry it all out and give it a pokier bitterness and you're in the Session IPA zone. Given their rate of growth and their now-ubiquitous presence in bars and bottles shops, we're all in the Session IPA zone.

My favorite gin

Hendrick's

Magic mystery mojito cocktail

So why not celebrate with a mysterious take on the classic? What's the mystery, you ask? There's no rum in this refreshing drink… just Hendrick's and St. Germaine. Magical Mystery Mojito Ingredients: ¾ oz Monin cucumber syrup 8-10 mint leaves 1½ oz Hendrick's gin ½ oz St. Germaineelderflower liqueur 1 oz lime juice. Directions: Muddle Monin cucumber syrup and mint leaves five times. Shake remaining ingredients with ice. Top with club soda. Garnish with mint sprig and cucumber spear.
